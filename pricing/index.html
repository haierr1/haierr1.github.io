Questions, answered

Do I need a data team or SQL knowledge?

No. Northwind writes the analysis for you in plain language. If you can read an email, you can use it.

Where is my data stored?

On EU infrastructure in Frankfurt, Germany. We offer a signed Data Processing Agreement and never sell or share your data.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are live in under five minutes with a single snippet or a warehouse connection. No tracking plan required upfront.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month, cancel in one click, and you can export all of your data on the way out.

Is there a free trial?

Every plan starts with a 14-day free trial. No credit card needed to begin.
